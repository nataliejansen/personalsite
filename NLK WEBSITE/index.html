Natalie Kuechler | Web Developer


Creating a professional yet unique web experience since 2022.

Welcome to my page! I am currently participating in a Full Stack Coding Program at Centriq and have a current graduation date of January 20th, 2023. My passion for web development stems back to my teenage years when I had began to teach myself HTML by utilizing personal social media pages such as Myspace and Xanga. I loved being able to use code to manipulate the design of the page and thought it was a fascinating way to make it look however I wanted. I then began taking web development classes in High School and the passion grew. I ended up pressing pause to develop my customer service skills at a few different restaurants. After about 8 years in the industry, I moved over to retail where I developed my customer service skills further as a sales associate at a local furniture store. My creative flame re-ignited as I was reminded how much I love design. A few years later I was promoted to a Store Manager and then managed at 3 of the 4 different locations over a span of about 4 years. In the middle of 2022 I had decided I was ready to move in a different direction with my career and recalled the passion I had for web development at a young age. I decided to pursue that passion and am happy to be here sharing it with you!
I look forward to helping your image come to life on the web.
